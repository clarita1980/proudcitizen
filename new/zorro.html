In-home Observation for Zorro

October 2008 - Buenos Aires, Argentina - Research commissioned by Ipsos.

Etnographic Researcher

Project definition, goals and objectives

The primary goal of the research was to gain insights into the laundry process from users and non-users of the brand. Secondary goal included the identification of parameters where the laundry process begins and ends and gaining an understanding of the emotional aspects behind clean and dirty clothes.


Process: methodos and techniques

We requested the client qualitative and quantitative studies from the category to be able to contextualize the present study.We performed 8 in depth interviews and observations of women using Zorro cloth washing product or competitors brand. We video recorded the interviews and used them to contextualize the lifes of the users.

Highlights:

  • Clean clothing speaks about your identity and competency as a wife, as a mother and someone responsible for your family's well-being.
  • The need for clothing to be washed goes beyond seeing the clothing as dirty. The passing of the time requires washing it.
  • The clothing that has one stain is not necessarily seen as dirty however it requires washing. This means that usually much more clothing is being washed than what advertising promotes for its products.
