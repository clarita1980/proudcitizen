Project definition, goals and objectives

Lean Roadmap began as project for a UX design course in GA. As an entrepreneur I hear all the time that entrepreneurs want to follow the lean methodology, specifically since it is a cheap and fast way for learning if there is product/market fit, but they get stuck in the customer development process when they don't know what to test, how to do it and how to measure it. They want to be able to build a feedback loop and scale it. Lean roadmap intends to be a preliminary solution as an online step-by-step customer development process following the startup Lean Methodology and available to help entrepreneurs figure out what and when to test and how to measure results and move on to the next step.


Process: methods and techniques

  • In depth interviews and observation with entrepreneurs.
  • Building Personas.
  • Mental model
  • Site maps
  • Sketchs and wireframes
  • Prototyping.
  • Visual design.
